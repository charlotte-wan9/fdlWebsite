OUR SHOWS

During the spring of 2022, our board members gathered and watched a original Chinese musical "An Endless Poetry".

They were so moved by the emotions and the themes of this musical and decided to re-create it.

Fall of that year, we successfully put on the show on our stage in Wisconsin,

and it was an incredible experience for all of our crew.

So, we are now planning to have a production every academic year,

either adapted or original, either Broadway or pop music,

we will be giving our all to provide the best theatre experience for musical ethusiasts from all over the world.
